This site was made to scratch my own itch. In my experience sites to find volunteering opportunities are enormously demotivating instead of uplifting.

I came across terrible UI experience while looking for volunteering opportunities in Amsterdam, The Netherlands a few years ago, from a company perspective, found the same to be true in Sydney, Australia, and while on a sabbatical the same seems to hold true for New Zealand.

So here I am being the change I seek! I'm not a frontend dev nor designer and by no means claim it looks great, at least you'll be able, or so I hope, what you find, quicker. If you want to help out improving this project, check out the collaboration page.
