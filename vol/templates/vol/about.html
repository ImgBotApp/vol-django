Vol was conceived by Otto Jongerius to fill his own need for a simple, user-friendly site to find volunteering opportunities.

Though he's not a front-end developer or designer, the clean, simple look is designed to connect you to charities and volunteering options faster and with the least fuss possible.

From humble beginnings here in Wellington, New Zealand, the plan is to expand the site until it has global reach.

If you have any suggestions or want to collaborate for improving this project, please check out the collaboration page.
